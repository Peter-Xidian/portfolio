About Me

A bit about me

From a very young age, I have had an obessive fascination with technology and software. I would take things like the Television, DVD players, and radios apart just to understand what made them work and try to couple them back together again. Think up different ways to make their design more appealing or improve the user experience (of course then I thought I was just making the DVD player more fun to use and Yes, I got into a lot of trouble as a kid).

As I grew, my fascination evolved into user interaction with technologies or software, I developed a strong desire to create a seamless interactive experience for users' relationships with solutions or software without impeding on its capabilities or its efficiency.

In the face of several paths to attain this goal, I chose Web Development (Frontend Development) and I have never enjoyed working more.
